About Us

Lavender Cleaning was founded in 1996 and has evolved to become one of the fastest growing Hurstville office cleaners in the metro area.

We are now a leading provider of cleaning services to the commercial, strata, office, residential, education, fitness, government, hospitality, resources and retail industries.

​We guarantee to address any problems within 2 hours. Lavender Cleaning’s Hurstville office cleaners work anytime; 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.
